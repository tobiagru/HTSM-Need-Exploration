Data privacy

We guarantee careful treatment of your personal data. We will treat it with high confidentiality and collect and process it according to this guideline.

By using our services you consent that we may treat and collect your data according to this guideline.

Collection & storage

By using the website the following usage data may be collected and stored by us:

  • Data, which are needed for your registration (e-mail address and your self-defined password) as well as service-specific adjustments (e.g. your preferences) and data that you provide on a voluntary basis (e.g. your name or your profile picture)
  • Data, which arise via usage of the website but which are not actively disclosed (e.g. your browser, your operating system and your IP-address)

There will be no forwarding of data to third parties with the exception of the Young Entrepreneur Club and the ETH Entrepreneur Club which will use the data solely for statistical investigation.

Additionally, you agree that specific contents (job advertisements) may be included to websites of third parties.

Cookies

During the usage of our website needseeker.io, permanent cookies and session-cookies will be created locally on your computer. Session-cookies are being deleted when closing the browser. Permanent cookies are being created when logging-in and make sure that you will be recognized when visiting our website again.

Deletion of your account & information on your data

If you would like to delete your account please send us an e-mail to info@needseeker.io containing the e-mail address belonging to your account at our website.

You are authorized to receive information on your own data storage by us. To do so, again please send us your request to info@needseeker.io.

Consent to changes

Our current data privacy is valid under https://www.needseeker.io/privacy.

Please check the data privacy regularly. By continuous use of our services you agree to possible changes in our data privacy.
